About Me

I have been a massive Marvel Comics fan since I was very young. Some of my earliest memories of Spider-Man are dressing up as him for halloween at 4 years old. Since then I have kept up my passion by reading countless comics, watching the Marvel Cinematic Universe develop into what we all know today, and spending many hours researching the heroes I love. I hope you all can get as much enjoyment from this website as I had making it.
